My Top Three Personal Values

Integrity

Integrity is an important part of my character as it builds trust with people, strengthens my reputation, and encourages accountability on my part.

I developed this value by listening to my parents as often as I could, learning discipline, boundaries, and socializing a lot during adolescence.

Integirty is important to uphold, as if you don't, others may no longer believe in you or count on you, whether in friendships, family, or work.

Hard Work

Hard work is a key driver in reaching personal, academic, or professional goals. Through hard work, people improve their abilities and gain experience.

I came to realize hard work is important when I realized after I was shot, that life will throw everything it can at me, and failure is part of learning. I took my experience and made it into something to drive me further.

If you don't vitalize hard work, you end up with missed potential. You limit your own growth, skills, and accomplishments by avoiding challenge or effort.

Compassion

Compassion creates bonds between people. When we show care, others feel valued and understood, which strengthens relationships.

I developed compassion by putting myself in others' shoes, seeing that if I was in their situation, I would want people to come to my aid as well.

People feel dismissed, misunderstood, or hurt when compassion is absent. I can create cold or toxic environments, which can truly bring a person down.

Self-Discipline

Self-Discipline creates people who are self-sufficient, have individuality, and are able to support not only themselves, but other people as well.

I learned self-discipline when I was shot and in the hospital, and had to learn how to adjust to my new life. Even though I would be sad, stiff, and acting out most days because I was truly hurting inside, but I realized that I can't make my pain, everyone else's pain.

Without discipline, goals remain dreams. There's little progress or growth. Undisciplined behavior (e.g., emotional outbursts, unreliability) strains trust and connection.
