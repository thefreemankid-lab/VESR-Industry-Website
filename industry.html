Industry Description

Intel operates in the semiconductor and technology industry, designing and manufacturing microprocessors, chipsets, and integrated circuits that power modern computing.

Career Role

As a hardware engineer, I design, test, and optimize physical components like CPUs and embedded systems, collaborating across teams to drive innovation in chip architecture.

Ethical Issue

One ethical concern is the potential misuse of design data, which could compromise Intel's property and competitive edge.

Cybersecurity Issues

Supply Chain Vulnerabilities: Risk of tampered firmware or embedded exploits during manufacturing.

Insider Threats: Exposure of sensitive design files through unsecured channels or intentional leaks.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Implement Zero Trust Architecture to verify all access requests.

Use Hardware Root of Trust for secure boot and firmware validation.

Conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.

Provide ongoing employee training on cybersecurity awareness.
